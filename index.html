Clean energy can also help reduce dependence on fossil energy sources which are limited and have unstable prices. Clean energy sources can be renewable energy sources, so they will not run out and can be used continuously.

However, the development and implementation of clean energy still faces several obstacles. One of them is the cost required to build the infrastructure and develop the technology needed to access and process clean energy sources. This cost can be an obstacle for countries that do not have sufficient financial resources.
